Frequently Asked Questions

Is C4Converter really free?
Yes, completely free. There are no hidden costs, premium tiers, or conversion limits. You can convert as many PDF files as you need without paying anything.
Do I need to create an account?
No. C4Converter works instantly without any registration, login, or account creation. Simply upload your PDF and convert.
Is my file secure?
Yes. Your files are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections, processed on secure servers, and permanently deleted immediately after conversion. We do not store, view, or share your documents.
How long are files kept on your servers?
Files are kept only during the brief processing period (typically 5-10 seconds) and are automatically deleted immediately after you download the converted file, or within 60 seconds maximum if not downloaded.
What is the maximum file size I can convert?
The current limit is 50MB per file. This accommodates most PDF documents while ensuring fast processing times for all users.
Will the conversion preserve my document formatting?
Our conversion process preserves text, images, tables, and basic formatting. However, PDF to DOCX conversion has inherent limitations - complex layouts may require minor manual adjustments in Word after conversion.
Can I convert password-protected PDFs?
Currently, password-protected PDFs cannot be converted. You'll need to remove the password protection first using PDF software before uploading.
Does this work with scanned PDFs?
Scanned PDFs (images of text) will convert to DOCX, but the text will not be editable unless the PDF has been processed with OCR (Optical Character Recognition) beforehand. Our converter does not perform OCR.
What browsers are supported?
C4Converter works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers on iOS and Android devices.
Can I use this on my phone or tablet?
Yes. Our converter is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets running any operating system.
Why use DOCX instead of DOC format?
DOCX is the modern Microsoft Word format (Word 2007 and later) and is now the industry standard. It offers better compression, improved formatting options, and wider compatibility with document editing software.
Can I edit the converted DOCX file?
Yes. DOCX files are fully editable in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LibreOffice Writer, and other word processors.
Is there a conversion limit per day?
No. There are no daily, weekly, or monthly limits. Convert as many files as you need.
Do you collect any user data or analytics?
No. We do not use analytics, tracking cookies, or any user monitoring. Your usage is completely private.
What happens if my conversion fails?
If a conversion fails, you'll see an error message explaining the issue. Common causes include corrupted PDFs, extremely large files, or network interruptions. Simply try uploading again or try a different file.
Can I convert multiple files at once?
Currently, files must be converted one at a time. However, there is no limit to how many sequential conversions you can perform.
Is there an API for developers?
Not at this time. C4Converter is designed as a web-based tool for end users. If you're interested in API access, please reach out through our contact channels.
Can I use this for commercial purposes?
Yes. You're free to use C4Converter for personal, educational, or commercial purposes as long as you comply with our Terms of Service.
