Frequently Asked Questions

Is a C4 file a real file format?
No. C4 is not a file format. "C4Converter" is simply our brand name for a PDF to DOCX conversion tool. There is no such thing as a "C4 file." We chose the name C4 because it's memorable and suggests converting "for" you.
What does C4Converter convert?
C4Converter converts PDF (Portable Document Format) files into editable Microsoft Word DOCX documents. That's it - PDF to DOCX only. This allows you to edit, modify, and reformat content in Word.
Why is it called C4Converter?
"C4" is our brand name. The "4" suggests converting "for" you, making it easy to remember. It's not related to any file format. We're a PDF to Word conversion tool with a catchy name.
Does C4Converter convert PDF to Word?
Yes! C4Converter converts PDF files to editable Microsoft Word DOCX format. Upload your PDF, and download an editable Word document.
Is C4Converter safe for confidential documents?
Yes. All files are uploaded securely via encrypted HTTPS connections, processed on secure servers, and automatically permanently deleted immediately after conversion. We do not store, view, or share your documents. No file history is maintained.
Do I need to install software to convert PDF to DOCX?
No. C4Converter is a fully browser-based tool that works on Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, and Android devices without requiring any downloads or plugins. Just open your browser and start converting.
Is C4Converter free to use?
Yes. C4Converter is 100% free with no hidden fees, no credit card requirements, and no daily limits on the number of conversions you can perform.
How long does PDF to DOCX conversion take?
Most conversions complete in just a few seconds. Larger files (up to 50MB) may take slightly longer depending on document complexity and your internet connection speed.
Can I convert password-protected PDFs?
Currently, password-protected PDFs cannot be converted. You'll need to remove the password protection first using PDF software before uploading to C4Converter.
Will the conversion preserve my document formatting?
Our conversion process preserves text, images, tables, and basic formatting. However, PDF to DOCX conversion has inherent limitations - complex layouts may require minor manual adjustments in Word after conversion.
What is the maximum file size I can convert?
The current limit is 50MB per file. This accommodates most PDF documents while ensuring fast processing times for all users.
How long are files kept on your servers?
Files are kept only during the brief processing period (typically 5-10 seconds) and are automatically deleted immediately after you download the converted file, or within 60 seconds maximum if not downloaded.
