Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Privacy Summary: C4Converter does not store your files, does not collect personal information, and does not track your activity. Your documents are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after conversion.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

C4Converter is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Payment information
  • Tracking cookies or analytics data
  • IP addresses for user profiling
  • Browser fingerprints

2. File Processing and Deletion

How We Handle Your Files:

  • Files uploaded to C4Converter are processed in temporary memory on our servers
  • Conversion happens immediately upon upload
  • Files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within seconds after conversion completes
  • We do not maintain any file history, backups, or archives
  • No one, including our administrators, has access to view or retrieve your documents after processing

3. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data during the brief processing period:

  • All file uploads are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Server-side processing occurs in isolated, secure environments
  • Automatic file deletion is enforced at the system level
  • No persistent storage mechanisms are used for user files

4. Third-Party Services

C4Converter does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies for user monitoring or analytics. Your browsing session is not tracked or recorded.

6. Data Retention

We practice a zero-retention policy:

  • Uploaded files: Deleted immediately after conversion (typically within 5-10 seconds)
  • Converted files: Deleted immediately after download or within 60 seconds, whichever comes first
  • User data: None collected, none retained

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your files at all times.

8. Children's Privacy

C4Converter does not knowingly collect data from users of any age. Our service does not require age verification, registration, or any form of personal information submission.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of C4Converter after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our official channels.
