What does C4Converter do?

1. PDF to DOCX: Convert PDF files into editable Microsoft Word documents with preserved formatting.

2. Merge PDFs: Combine up to 10 PDF files into a single document (max 100MB total).

3. Split PDF: Extract specific pages or page ranges (e.g., "1-5,8,10-12") from any PDF file.

4. PDF to Images: Convert PDF pages to PNG or JPG images with custom DPI quality (150-300).

Is C4Converter free and safe?

Yes. C4Converter is completely free with no hidden costs or subscription requirements. No user registration is needed. All files are processed in a secure server environment and automatically permanently deleted immediately after conversion. We do not store, view, or share your documents.
